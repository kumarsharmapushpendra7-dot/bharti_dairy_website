milk products list

Fluid Milk Products: Milk: This is the base product, available in various forms like whole milk, skim milk, and flavored milk. Buttermilk: A slightly tangy, fermented milk product. Yogurt: Fermented milk with a thick, creamy texture. Lassi: A popular Indian yogurt-based drink. Flavored milk: Milk with added flavors like chocolate or strawberry. Kefir: A fermented milk drink known for its tartness and probiotic benefits. Solid Milk Products: Butter: Made from churning cream. Cheese: A diverse category of milk products with varying textures and flavors, made through fermentation and curdling. Ice cream: A frozen dessert typically made with milk, cream, and sugar. Cream: The fatty portion of milk, used in various culinary applications. Ghee: Clarified butter, popular in South Asian cuisine. Condensed milk: Milk reduced by evaporation and sweetened. Dried milk: Milk with most of its water content removed, also known as milk powder. Khoa/Mawa: A milk-based solid, used in Indian sweets. Paneer: An Indian cheese, often used in curries. Kulfi: A traditional Indian frozen dessert, similar to ice cream but denser. Shrikhand: A sweet, strained yogurt dessert from India.
